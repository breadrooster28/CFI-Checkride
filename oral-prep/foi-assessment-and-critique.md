# FOI: Assessment and Critique

Total questions: 2

## 1. Compare formative versus summative assessment in flight training.

- **ACS Area/Task:** Area I — Fundamentals of Instructing — Task D: Assessment and Critique
- **Model answer:** Formative assessment occurs continuously during training and guides improvement; summative assessment evaluates whether final standards were met at the end of a stage or course.
- **FAA reference:** AIH FAA-H-8083-9 Ch. 5

## 2. What are characteristics of an effective oral critique after a flight?

- **ACS Area/Task:** Area I — Fundamentals of Instructing — Task D: Assessment and Critique
- **Model answer:** Objective, timely, specific, collaborative, and linked to standards; include strengths, deficiencies, root causes, and a concrete improvement plan.
- **FAA reference:** AIH FAA-H-8083-9 Ch. 5
