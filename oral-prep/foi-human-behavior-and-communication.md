# FOI: Human Behavior and Communication

Total questions: 4

## 1. A learner gets defensive when corrected. Which defense mechanism is likely, and how should you respond as an instructor?

- **ACS Area/Task:** Area I — Fundamentals of Instructing — Task A: Human Behavior and Effective Communication
- **Model answer:** Likely rationalization, denial, or projection. Keep feedback objective, focus on observable performance, ask reflective questions, and assign a concrete improvement plan.
- **FAA reference:** AIH FAA-H-8083-9 Ch. 2 and Ch. 3
- **Gotcha / DPE follow-up:** DPE follow-up: distinguish criticism of behavior from criticism of the person.
- **Scenario-based:** Yes

## 2. What are Maslow’s hierarchy levels and how do they affect lesson readiness?

- **ACS Area/Task:** Area I — Fundamentals of Instructing — Task A: Human Behavior and Effective Communication
- **Model answer:** Physiological, safety/security, belonging, esteem, and self-actualization. If lower needs are unmet, motivation and learning degrade, so instructors address basic comfort, stress, and psychological safety first.
- **FAA reference:** AIH FAA-H-8083-9 Ch. 2

## 3. Define the communication process and identify common barriers in flight training.

- **ACS Area/Task:** Area I — Fundamentals of Instructing — Task A: Human Behavior and Effective Communication
- **Model answer:** Source encodes message, receiver decodes, and feedback closes the loop. Barriers include lack of common experience, overuse of abstractions, interference, and confusion between symbol and symbolized object.
- **FAA reference:** AIH FAA-H-8083-9 Ch. 3

## 4. How do you reduce learner anxiety before first solo?

- **ACS Area/Task:** Area I — Fundamentals of Instructing — Task A: Human Behavior and Effective Communication
- **Model answer:** Set clear standards, use progressive exposure, brief contingencies, normalize stress, and ensure recent successful repetitions. Anxiety decreases when competence and predictability rise.
- **FAA reference:** AIH FAA-H-8083-9 Ch. 2; CFI ACS
- **Scenario-based:** Yes
