# FOI: Lesson Planning

Total questions: 2

## 1. What are the required parts of a complete lesson plan?

- **ACS Area/Task:** Area I — Fundamentals of Instructing — Task C: Course Development, Lesson Plans, and Classroom Training
- **Model answer:** Objective, standards, references, equipment/materials, elements/content, instructor and learner actions, schedule/time, completion standards, and evaluation/assignment.
- **FAA reference:** AIH FAA-H-8083-9 Ch. 6; CFI ACS
- **Gotcha / DPE follow-up:** Gotcha: completion standards must be measurable.

## 2. How do you adapt a lesson plan for a learner who is behind schedule?

- **ACS Area/Task:** Area I — Fundamentals of Instructing — Task C: Course Development, Lesson Plans, and Classroom Training
- **Model answer:** Keep standards unchanged, adjust pace and strategy: simplify tasks, chunk content, use targeted drills, assign remediation, and verify understanding before progression.
- **FAA reference:** AIH FAA-H-8083-9 Ch. 6
- **Scenario-based:** Yes
